# Project Proposal Template (Sample)

## 1. Executive Summary
Briefly describe the problem, the target population, the proposed solution, expected outcomes, budget summary, and project duration.

## 2. Context & Problem Statement
Provide background, needs assessment, and evidence supporting the problem significance.

## 3. Objectives & Theory of Change
- Overall Goal
- Specific Objectives
- Theory of Change diagram or narrative

## 4. Implementation Plan
- Activities and Workplan
- Roles & Responsibilities
- Risk Management

## 5. Monitoring, Evaluation, and Learning (MEL)
- Indicators (outcome/output)
- Data sources, collection methods, frequency
- Learning and adaptation plan

## 6. Safeguards & Inclusion
- Child protection, gender equality, disability inclusion
- Environmental and Social safeguards compliance

## 7. Budget & Value for Money
- Line-item budget (direct/indirect)
- Cost share and justification
- Procurement approach

## 8. Sustainability & Exit Strategy
How outcomes will be sustained post-grant.

## 9. Organizational Capacity
Relevant experience, governance, staffing, financial management.

## 10. Annexes
- Logical Framework (LogFrame)
- Workplan Gantt
- Letters of Support
