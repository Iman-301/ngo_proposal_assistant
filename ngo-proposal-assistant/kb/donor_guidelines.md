# Donor Guidelines (Sample)

## Eligibility
- Registered non-profit/NGO status required.
- Prior sector experience preferred (education, health, livelihoods, environment).
- Geographic focus: Sub-Saharan Africa and South Asia.

## Budget & Cost Share
- Maximum total budget: $250,000 per project.
- Cost share: Minimum 10% of total project cost (cash or in-kind).
- Indirect cost rate capped at 10% of direct costs.

## Monitoring & Evaluation (M&E)
- Logical Framework (LogFrame) required with outcomes, outputs, indicators.
- Baseline and endline assessments required.
- Quarterly progress reports with KPI updates and learning.

## Safeguards
- Environmental and Social safeguards screening required.
- Child protection and safeguarding policies must be in place.

## Procurement & Compliance
- Competitive procurement for purchases above $5,000.
- At least 3 quotes or a sole-source justification.
- Comply with anti-fraud and anti-corruption standards.

## Reporting
- Quarterly narrative and financial reports.
- Final evaluation report within 60 days of closeout.
- Audit may be required for grants above $100,000.
